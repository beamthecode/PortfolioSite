    About

    I am from a small town in Northeast Missouri. Growing up in a rural area I was naturally drawn to music, and it is to this day my biggest creative hobby. I attended Columbia College in Columbia, MO. There I majored in Marketing, graduating with a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration.

    Upon graduation I found myself working in an accounting department, having accepted an accounts payable position. I have always had a mind that was both creative and analytical, so I was able to adapt to the accounting world with relative ease. I now have eight years of experience working in accounting. It has been of tremendous assistance to me in terms of:

    • Problem Solving
    • Attention to Detail
    • Organizational Work Flow

    In December 2021 I started pursuing my goal of learning how to develop. Developing has always been of interest to me, as it is both a creative and analytical endeavor. I fell in love with the idea that we can create new applications that make our world easier. We can create platforms that people interact with. I love the idea that we can make life more accessible for other people.
